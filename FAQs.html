FAQs

1. Where is the Mukhtaran Rafiq Welfare Hospital located?

The Mukhtaran Rafiq Welfare Hospital is located at 146, Saeed Block Canal Bank Housing Scheme, Fatehgarh, Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan.

2. How can I contribute or donate to the Mukhtaran Rafiq Welfare Hospital?

There are several ways you can contribute to the Mukhtaran Rafiq Welfare Hospital. You can make a donation online through our website using a credit card or debit card or may can send donation to Mukhtaran Rafiq Foundation bank account. Additionally, you can visit our hospital in person and donate cash.

3. Which bank account can I use to send a donation online to the Mukhtaran Rafiq Welfare Hospital?

4. How does my donation impact the hospital?

Your contribution goes towards offering medical services to individuals in need, such as surgeries, dental care, physiotherapy, gynecological treatments, purchasing necessary medical equipment, and ensuring the upkeep of hospital facilities for the provision of high-quality care to all patients. Mukhtaran Rafiq Welfare Hospital caters to more than 500 patients every day with free medical services, and this has only been made achievable through the kind donations of supporters.

    • 5. Can I make a donation in honor or memory of a loved one?

    Yes, you can make a donation in honor or memory of a loved one in shape of equipment or facility. Please reach out to our official WhatsApp number at +92 309 7773177 or you may email us at info@mrf.org.pk for more information on how to proceed with this type of donation.

    6. How can I volunteer at the Mukhtaran Rafiq Welfare Hospital?

    We welcome volunteers at the Mukhtaran Rafiq Welfare Hospital. Whether you have a medical background or simply want to help others, you can inquire about volunteer opportunities by contacting our hospital's official WhatsApp number at +92 309 7773177 or you may email us at info@mrf.org.pk.

    7. How can I check the status of my donation?

    To inquire about your donation, please contact our official WhatsApp number at +92 309 7773177 or you may email us at info@mrf.org.pk. We will provide you with information on the status of your donation and how it has been utilized along with your donation slip.

    8. What medical services are offered at the Mukhtaran Rafiq Welfare Hospital?

    The Mukhtaran Rafiq Welfare Hospital offers a variety of medical & surgical consultation including emergency care with emergency surgeries, surgical procedures, maternal and child health services, and general medical consultations. Our mission is to provide comprehensive healthcare to all patients in need.

    9. Can I sponsor a specific program or patient at the hospital?

    Yes, you can sponsor a specific program or patient at the Mukhtaran Rafiq Welfare Hospital. There are various sponsorship opportunities available, such as sponsoring a surgery, funding a medical outreach program, providing financial assistance to a patient in need, sponsoring daily free meal, monthly grocery bags, sponsoring a dialysis patient and more. Please contact our official WhatsApp number at +92 309 7773177 or you may email us at info@mrf.org.pk for more information on sponsoring opportunities.

    10. Can I tour the Mukhtaran Rafiq Welfare Hospital to see the work being done?

    Certainly! We would be delighted to offer you a tour of our hospital so you can see firsthand the impactful social welfare work we are doing for the betterment of our community. We warmly welcome all guests and are eager to showcase the dedication and compassion of our healthcare team. Please feel free to reach out to hospital's official WhatsApp number at +92 309 7773177 to schedule a visit at your convenience.
